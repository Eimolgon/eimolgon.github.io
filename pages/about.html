About me

Who am I? Hello! I'm Benjamin.
I grew up in a small town called Puerto Varas at the beginning of Patagonia in Chile. During my whole life, I have been surrounded by cars and motorcycles. The interest for these vehicles is the cornerstone in my life, to the point that my WhatsApp status says 'If I don't answer, it's because I'm riding my bike', which is quite accurate and represents my philosophy.
Studying a PhD in bicycle dynamics comes from the curiosity of understanding something as commonplace as a bicycle, together with the idea of having a job/life closely related to what I love to do.
When people ask me why I decided to come here, I answer in the same way Forrest Gump answered to the question of why he ran: "I just wanted to ride a bike".
So, in short, I'm a bike maniac and with a deep curiosity for understanding what surrounds me (from a mechanical point of view).
